Mental Health Checkup

A broad assessment tool to help you understand your overall mental wellness, covering mood, stress, energy, and focus.

1. Over the last 2 weeks, how often have you felt down, depressed, or hopeless?
2. How often have you been unable to stop or control worrying or felt overwhelmed by stress?
3. Have you had trouble falling or staying asleep, or sleeping too much?
4. How often have you felt tired, fatigued, or had very little energy?
5. Have you had trouble concentrating on things, such as work, reading, or watching television?
6. How often have you felt little interest or pleasure in doing things you usually enjoy?

Wellness & Coping Strategies

🧘

Mindfulness Meditation

Spend 5-10 minutes daily focusing on your breath to help ground your thoughts and reduce stress.

💤

Sleep Hygiene

Maintain a consistent sleep schedule and create a relaxing environment to improve your energy and mood.

🏃

Active Movement

Physical activity releases endorphins that can naturally boost your mood and focus.

🧠

Professional Support

If symptoms persist, speaking with a licensed therapist can provide valuable tools for emotional well-being.

Disclaimer: This mental health checkup is for informational and educational purposes only. It is NOT a clinical diagnosis or a replacement for professional medical advice. Mental wellness is complex and these results only offer a snapshot. If you are experiencing persistent low mood, overwhelming stress, or thoughts of self-harm, please contact a qualified mental health professional or emergency services immediately.
