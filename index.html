Frequently Asked Questions

Is HealthBot AI a doctor? +

No. It provides general disease awareness and prevention education only. It does not diagnose or prescribe treatments.

Can the chatbot give a diagnosis? +

No. The assistant avoids diagnoses and medicine recommendations. Use the information to learn, then consult a clinician for personal evaluation.

How does symptom checking work? +

The app maps selected symptoms to broad educational categories and safety tips based on established health data.

What is the report analysis feature? +

You can upload a medical report in the chatbot. The AI will explain findings in plain language for educational purposes only.

How do I use location-based health alerts? +

Logged-in users get automatic info on local Air Quality Index (AQI) and health outbreaks based on their profile location or detected area.
